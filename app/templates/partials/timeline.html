1977

Ordained a Catholic priest.

1977-1981

Assistant pastor St. John the Evangelist, Lower Makefield, Bucks County.

1981-1984

Associate pastor, St. Cecilia’s, Northeast Philadelphia.

1984

Removed from St. Cecilia’s by the Archdiocese of Philadelphia after he admits to sexually abusing boys at the Bucks County parish. Sent to a Catholic treatment facility, where he is declared a pedophile.

1985

Walks out on treatment and leaves ministry, and walks away. Archdiocese does not notify law enforcement or parishioners about his sexual misconduct.

1985 or 1986

Takes a high school teaching job in the Diocese of Metuchen, N.J.

1987-1992

Owns house on 1200 block of Panama Street in Center City.

1993-2000

Runs a child’s birthday party business in Philadelphia while owning a house on the 3400 block of Midvale Avenue in East Falls.

2002-2007

Lives in Virginia. Arrested in 2002 on charges of molesting a teenager. The case is dismissed.

Sometime between 2002 and 2005

Called to appear before Philadelphia grand jury investigating sex clergy abuse. Refuses to answer questions.

2005

Grand jury report unmasks him as one of 63 priests credibly accused of sexually abusing children in Philadelphia archdiocese. Estimates he could have victimized more than 100 children. Defrocked by Catholic Church.

2009-2010

Has addresses in West Hollywood, Calif. and Kenosha, Wisc.; arrested for drunken driving in New Mexico.

2014

Run out of a Dallas residence after neighbors realized his past as an alleged pedophile ex-priest. Moved to a different Dallas apartment complex.

September 2017

Abandons his Dallas apartment after neighbors are told about his past as a sexually abusive priest.

Sept. 13

Found dead of a heart attack in Fort Worth motel.
